Meet Me

I am a Data Analyst seeking an opportunity to help solve challenges with data, curate stories, and bring enormous value using tools such as SQL, Tableau, Power BI in business, finance, food security, health, education and sometimes music.

My journey into data analysis started with a simple curiosity about the data I was collecting and analyzing for my myocardial infarction (heart attack) prediction research. I wanted to know more about the data, how it was collected, and how I could use it to tell a story. I started with Excel and SPSS (and a bit of R), then added SQL, Tableau, Power BI and currently learning Python.

This foray into research led me to a huge fascination with data which even became more apparent during the covid years when quality data, proper analysis, honest data interpretation, and effective communication were sought after.

Long story short, I ensured that every other role or work I took on involved data, no matter the size - I was hooked. And now, similar to when I plateaued in learning the piano by myself, I am on the look out for a bigger challenge.
